Our Mission

Individuals have not hit the limits of their potential. Groups of individuals are even farther from their potential.

Step by step, we do the hard work to deliver working products that empower everyone to continuously dream bigger, even in the most complex scenarios.

We let every individual dream bigger. We let every organization dream bigger. We dream bigger.

Windsurf has been developed by a team of researchers and engineers to build the future of software development.

We realized that the combination of recent advances in generative models and our world-class optimized deep learning serving software could provide users with top quality AI-based products at the lowest possible costs (or ideally, free!).

The result of that realization is Windsurf.
