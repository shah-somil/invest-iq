Careers at DataVisor

Talented and driven individuals with a commitment to customer success and apassion for building a safe, secure, and fraud-free world are invited to explorecareers with DataVisor.

Our Mission

Founded in 2013, our mission is to build and restore trust online. We provide an end-to-end fraud and risk SaaS platform that is powered by AI and advanced machine learning for financial institutions and large organizations to combat a variety of different types of frauds and money laundering activities, all in real time. We empower our customers to fight fraud and financial crimes in a much more proactive manner, with automation, efficiency, and scalability, providing a safe and trustworthy experience to their end users and customers.

Our Culture

The DataVisor team is composed of a diverse group of individuals united around a common goalempowering our customers to protect their organizations and communities against fraud and abuse. We take a results-driven approach to achieving excellence on behalf of our customers, and prioritize team spirit and hard work. DataVisor employees are honest, curious, and maintain the highest levels of integrity.

The DataVisor Community
