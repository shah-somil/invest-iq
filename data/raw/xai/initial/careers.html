[ What to expect ]

Interview process

We generally do not use recruiters for assessments. Applications are evaluated by our technical team members. Interviews will be conducted via virtual meetings and/or onsite.

Step 1

Submit your application

Our team will review your CV and statement of exceptional work.

Apply Now
Step 2

Screening Interview

A short interview to learn more about you and assess if the role fits both you and xAI. Prepare for short technical questions and a review of your background and experience.

Step 3

Technical Interviews

Sessions where we dive deep into your technical expertise. This isn't just about solving puzzles; it's about understanding how you approach complex problems, your coding style, and your ability to think critically under pressure.

Step 4

Offer Extended

We value transparency and efficiency, so expect a prompt decision. If you've demonstrated the exceptional skills and mindset we're looking for, we'll extend an offer to join xAI. This isn't just a job offer; it's an invitation to be part of a team that's pushing the boundaries of AI to accelerate human scientific discovery.

[ Offices ]

Collaboration across borders

We are hiring across all our offices in our Palo Alto headquarters, Seattle, San Francisco, Tennessee, and London UK. We prioritize in-person work to support our fast-paced, collaborative projects. Exceptional candidates are welcome to apply from anywhere.
