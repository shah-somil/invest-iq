Were on a mission to make running a small business easier

Founded in 2019 by Lauren Myrick, Connor Dunn, and a team with deep experience in taxes, accounting, and financial services, Found provides the support and structure that small business owners need to thrive.

Running a business can be infinitely rewarding, but it comes with a host of challengesfrom bookkeeping to invoicing to taxes. With business banking, automated expense tracking, tax estimation, and smart financial tools all in one platform, Found helps small business owners have more time to do what they do best.
