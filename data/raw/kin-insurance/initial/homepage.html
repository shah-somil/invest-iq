Frequently asked questions about Kin

How long does it take to get a home insurance quote?

On average, it takes less than three minutes to generate a personalized home insurance quote online with Kin. It’s fast, easy, and you’ll be able to customize coverage limits, endorsements, and deductibles to balance the protection you need with a price that’s right for you.

Prefer to work with a licensed agent? Get connected by chat, email, or over the phone.

Can I switch my existing insurance through Kin?

Yes, and we can help. Most insurance companies allow you to cancel your current policy at any time and will issue you a refund.

Just enter your property address or call us to get started.

What mortgage lenders accept policies offered by Kin?

The policies we offer are accepted by most major mortgage lenders. Our carrier partners have earned a Financial Stability Rating® (FSR) of A, Exceptional from Demotech, Inc. — so whether you’re buying a new home or insuring a property you already own, you can rest assured your policy will have you covered. 

The most current Financial Stability Rating® must be verified by visiting www.demotech.com.

Can I speak with a licensed insurance agent?

We know how important it can be to speak with a human instead of a robot, so our licensed agents are available to answer your insurance questions and help you find the right coverage Monday through Saturday over the phone, email, or chat. We're happy to help you get a quote or manage your existing policy. 

For claims, we’re available online or by phone 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year.

Can I manage my policy online?

The Kin Customer Portal makes it easy to view your policy, payment history, and other documents at any time. Customers can also submit and download policy documents, update payment methods, make payments, and track claims.

How do I file a claim?

We’re here for you when disaster strikes. Claims can be filed online or over the phone with a Kin customer support representative. Our team is here to support you 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year. 

Once your claim is filed, you can track its progress at any time in the Kin Customer Portal.

For properties located outside of Florida or Louisiana, Kin "homeowners insurance" and "home insurance" refers to the Kin House & Property policy with an owner-occupied endorsement added. The base Kin House & Property policy, the HD3 policy, provides the coverages for customers who rent out their home to others, similar to a DP3 policy. For customers who live in their home full time or part time, an owner-occupied endorsement is added to provide the coverages of a typical homeowners insurance policy, which is similar to a HO3 policy.
