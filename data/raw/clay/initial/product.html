Clay helps go-to-market teams up level their data enrichment and automate personalized outreach. You can start with a list of leads, either prospected through Clay or from your own CRM, and follow the steps below.

Step 1: Basic Data Enrichment & Cleaning

Clay combines 75+ data enrichment tools into one marketplace, resulting in superior data coverage and qualityâon anything from contact information to company firmographics. Our customers have tripled data coverage vs. providers like Zoominfo while cutting costs by a magnitude of ten or twenty.

Step 2: AI Data Enrichment

We enable you to use AI to do research that previously required manual SDR work, including reading and analyzing webpages, PDFs, and Google searches for specific information.

Step 3: Automated Outreach

Once youâve nailed your data enrichment, you can use AI to craft relevant, compelling messaging based on that data. Everything syncs back to your CRM, allowing your team to go from campaign idea to execution in seconds.

How does Clay impact the conversation about stricter spam limits?

There are more APIs and data enrichment tools than ever before and more SaaS companies selling to other SaaS companies as well. Clay helps companies find the right customers and message them at the right time, which means sending a smaller number of highly targeted emails rather than large blasts of generic, spammy messages.

Clay helps companies avoid spam filters by actually sending relevant, compelling emails. This is better for companies and better for customers.
