Questions and Answers

What is Pollo AI?

Pollo AI is an ultimate, all-in-one AI video and image generator that allows you to easily create stunning videos and images from various inputs. With Pollo AI, you can transform your ideas into high quality, engaging videos/images in any style promptly.

How can I get started with Pollo AI?

Pollo AI offers a novice-friendly UI and streamlined generation process. Just provide your text/image/video inputs, configure your customization options and start the generation. You'll get the generated images/videos tailored to your needs. If you need help using Pollo AI, you can join our Discord server https://discord.gg/zyxEJfsF7u.

How fast is Pollo AI video generator?

Pollo AI video generator is designed for speed. While the exact time can vary depending on the complexity of your input and requirements, as well as the model you choose, Pollo AI can provide significantly faster speed than our competitors.

Why should I use Pollo AI image generator?

Pollo AI lets you create images from text prompts, existing images, or chat conversations. You get access to many top industry AI models like GPT-4o. It also includes over 2,000 specialized LoRA models, 100+ specialized image generators, and more, all on one platform.

Do I need any technical skills to use Pollo AI avatar generator?

Not at all! There is no pretraining or pre-recording requiredsimply upload a photo, add your script or audio, and the AI instantly creates a lifelike, speaking avatar with natural expressions and gestures. You can also try the Pollo AI product avatar generator to create product ad videos instantly.

How does Pollo AI ensure the uniqueness of my generated video or image?

The AI technology of Pollo AI is designed to generate unique content based on the specific inputs you provide. This ensures that every video and image created with Pollo AI is original and tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

Do Pollo AI-generated images/videos have watermarks?

If you're a free plan user, the images or videos you generate with Pollo AI will come with a watermark. To get watermark-free outputs, you'll need to subscribe to one of our paid plans.

Can I use Pollo AI for commercial purposes?

Yes, videos or images created with Pollo AI can be used for commercial purposes, if you're a paid plan subscriber. Whether you're using it in your advertisement, promotional content, or a product explainer video, Pollo AI is equipped to meet your needs.

Is Pollo AI free to use?

Yes, Pollo AI offers a free plan! This allows users to explore its features and create images or videos without any cost. Upgrade options are also available if you need more generation credits, advanced features and capabilities.
