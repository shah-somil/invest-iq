Propel's mission:

To build technology that strengthens the social safety net in America.

We envision a future where every American has the tools and resources they need to thrive.

Financial volatility is a fact of life. And when people face financial hardship, they’re met with tools that are outdated, broken, and hard to navigate.

We founded Propel to build technology that makes people’s government benefits better, at the speed and scale they deserve.

And years later, that’s still our mission. We spend every single day championing the people we serve, filling gaps when the safety net falls short, and using the power of technology to solve real life problems, right now.
