Solve real problems for real people

Technology for all#technology-for-all

We believe everyone deserves access to user-friendly technology that improves lives. We empower low-income Americans by simplifying the complexities of America’s safety net and identify other opportunities to strengthen their financial well-being.

We're not just building a product: we're helping pave the way for a more inclusive tech landscape.

Unique challenges, rewarding impact#unique-challenges-rewarding-impact

We’re building a large, sustainable, and profitable company. Achieving our mission is intrinsically linked to the success of our business. And there’s no playbook for this. But at Propel, we’re leading the charge, and our team of thoughtful, creative builders are comfortable with uncertainty and up for the challenge.

Justice, equity, diversity, and inclusivity

Building a diverse team isn't just the right thing to do: it's essential. We’re actively working to ensure fair and equitable processes for everyone—from hiring and compensation decisions to growth and promotion opportunities.

We're a growing company, and achieving a representative team takes time. We recognize that the tech industry hasn't always excelled at inclusivity, so we're committed to doing better by building a team that reflects the diversity of our users.

Our values

Mission-driven excellence

We aim for quality in our craft, because our mission—and our users—deserve nothing less.

Progress over perfection

We believe in the power of small improvements that build over time. We take pride in progress.

Listen first

We approach our work with humility and admit that there's a lot we don't know.

Propel each other

We win and learn as a team. We grow by pushing each other to do our best work.

Our team

Team members have the opportunity to do what they love while making a social impact. We’re a team of smart, motivated, and caring people.

Benefits & perks

Competitive compensation

We offer total compensation packages that include cash, equity, and benefits. Our salary bands target the 75th percentile of the market.

Remote-first

Team members work remotely across the US. We also have offices in Brooklyn, Salt Lake City, and San Francisco. We bring everyone together once or twice a year.

Generous benefits

We cover up to 99% of the premium for medical, dental, and vision. We also have 16-week parental leave, 20 vacation days, and 401k matching eligibility on day 1.
